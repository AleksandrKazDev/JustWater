# App Store Metadata — JustWater

## Primary Language: English

### App Name
JustWater

### Subtitle
Track daily water intake

### Promotional Text
Build a calmer hydration habit with simple daily water tracking, widgets, reminders, and Apple Health sync.

### Description
JustWater is a simple and calm hydration tracker for iPhone.

Set a daily hydration goal, quickly log water and other drinks, review your history, use Home Screen widgets, sync your entries with Apple Health, and track how consistently you reach your goal.

Features:
- Daily water intake tracking
- Quick add buttons
- Custom drink entries
- Drink type selection
- Daily goal calculator
- History by day, week, month, and year
- Goal completion statistics
- Home Screen widgets
- Apple Health sync
- ml and fl oz support
- Local hydration reminders
- Light, dark, and system appearance modes
- English and Russian localization

JustWater is designed for general wellness tracking and is not medical advice.

Your data stays on your device. JustWater does not require an account and does not use ads, analytics, or tracking.

### What's New
JustWater 1.1 adds Apple Health sync, Home Screen widgets, ml / fl oz support, and improvements to History, performance, and overall stability.

### Notes
Hydration entries, goal history, reminder settings, appearance settings, haptics settings, onboarding state, widget data, Apple Health sync preference, measurement unit preference, and app preferences are stored locally on the user's device.

The app does not send this data to external servers.

When Apple Health sync is enabled, JustWater writes logged water intake to Apple Health using HealthKit. This sync is optional and controlled by the user.
