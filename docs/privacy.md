# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

JustWater is designed for simple hydration tracking.

## Data Storage

JustWater stores hydration entries, goal history, reminder settings, appearance settings, haptics settings, onboarding state, and app preferences locally on your device.

The app does not send this data to any external server.

## Data Collection

JustWater does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

## Notifications

JustWater can schedule local hydration reminders if you enable notifications.

Notification settings are stored locally on your device. Reminders are used only to help you maintain your hydration habit.

## Health Disclaimer

JustWater is intended for general wellness tracking only and is not medical advice.
